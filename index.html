As more EVs hit the road, the charging network to support them must also grow. Our solution for residential customers makes their personal EV charger publicly available at a fee. This will allow the network to grow while providing an incentive for users to use our product.

What we do

Help broaden Canada's EV grid

We get it, having an electric car has its own set of challenges. The EV infrastrucutre is not yet caught up with the current gas powered one. This can cause some serious range anxiety as Canada is a very large travelers are often faced with long drives. By adopting Flux, we ensure that every electric car owner becomes a new node on the grid.

Create an account, search for a station and reserve within seconds with our clean modern UI. Seamless integration with Paypal for safe and secure transactions. View your charging statistics, leave reviews or add your own charger. Search for Flux on Google Play today.
